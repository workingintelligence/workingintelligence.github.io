Shelf Help - augmented and open carpentry

by Rachel Lane, Taishi Kamiya and Bora Kim

Smart technology isn’t about tools that do the work for us, it’s about tools that work with us. DITT (Do It Together Technology) is a suite of open-source tools designed to take the tedium out of carpentry and empower people in their own home.

Shelf Help is a consumer-friendly digital chalk line and blueprint augmentation. Shelf Help will work with you to put up a shelf, offering full personalisation in the app and layered instructions on any surface where you will be working.
